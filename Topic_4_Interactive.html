SUCCESS STRATEGIES FOR POLYNOMIAL OPERATIONS & ADVANCED FACTORING

For Students

  • Show ALL work on constructed response questions - this is where partial credit comes from!
  • Use proper mathematical vocabulary in your explanations
  • Check your work by multiplying factored expressions back out

Common Student Errors to Avoid:

  • Forgetting to factor out the GCF first before other factoring techniques
